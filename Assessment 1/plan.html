Goal

Build a small website for 100SMILES Dental Care that measurably increases new-patient enquiries and bookings from local families (children’s first visits) and retirees in Razole, Queensland. Specifically, within the first 4 weeks after launch, achieve: (1) ≥20% more email booking enquiries leveraging the “20% first visit” promotion; (2) ≥30 unique phone tap-to-call clicks per week during trading hours; and (3) ≥40 reads of at least one educational article per week to support preventive-care awareness. These goals align to the clinic’s “all ages” positioning, long-standing local presence (33 Fantail Street, Razole), broad service range (check-ups, cleanings, implants, orthodontics, cosmetic), and current promotions.:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
