Tips for Maintaining Oral Hygiene

  • Brush gently twice a day and clean along the gumline.
  • Floss once a day to remove plaque between teeth.
  • Use fluoride toothpaste and drink water regularly.
  • Limit sugary snacks and acidic drinks between meals.
  • Schedule routine cleanings and check-ups with your dentist.
